## About

I am Aryan Boruah, a master's student at IISER Thiruvananthapuram.

My research interests lie at the intersection of:

- Mathematical biology
- Morphogenesis
- Collective cell behavior
- Computational modelling of developmental systems

I am interested in developing mathematical and computational frameworks to understand emergent biological pattern formation.
