# Computational Modelling 
> Worked on agent-based modeling, where I studied the role of stromal cell dynamics in epithelial tissue folding in the mouse uterus using CompuCell3D. I developed a model to simulate epithelial folding in response to a morphogen and investigated how perturbations in stromal behavior influence epithelial topology.
## PI: Dr Ripla Arora
## Institute: Michigan State University

# Single cell RNA sequence analysis 
> I have prior experience in scRNA seq analysis. My project was focused on investigating cellular diversity and developmental patterns by comprehensive analysis of scRNA seq data from developing mice and rats. I have applied to the SEURAT pipeline to analyse the single cell data. I explored and applied SAMap algorithm for cross-species data comparison.
## PI: Dr Marketa Kaucka
## Institute: Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Biology

# Computational Metagenomics 
> To investigate the relationship between cardiometabolic markers such as Glucose, Triglycerides, HDL, and LDL- and gut bacteria, I conducted a comprehensive metagenomic analysis using data curated from 20 independent studies. These studies, derived from various research papers, provided patient-specific metagenomic profiles, with the markers serving as target variables and the gut bacterial abundances as features. we developed a custom script to explore these associations. I implemented a supervised machine learning approach using Random
Forest models to evaluate each study data for every marker.
## PI: Dr Tarini Shankar Ghosh
## Institute: IIIT Delhi
